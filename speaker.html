Speakers and Judges

Learn more about our speakers and judges

Matthew Wells

General Manager, GE Automation Software
Matthew has led the GE automation software division for the last 2-years and has been instrumental in the development of the automation software product line. Matt has 20 years of experience with industrial automation and operations management software. Matt has worked with customers in a wide variety of industries including life sciences, food and beverage, automotive, metals, and pulp and paper.




James Bateman

CEO & Co-founder, Medchart
James is a life-long-learner, passionate entrepreneur, and avid programmer. He began MedChart after experiencing on many occasions the need to access and share medical records within his family. He brings experience as the founder of three prior startups and from working on hospital health data systems. James holds a Master of Applied Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering and a Bachelor in Engineering Science. When James is not plotting the next quantum revolution, he may be found building a house as a nationally licensed Carpenter.




Alexis Smirnov

Alexis is co-founder & CTO of Dialogue, a telemedicine service that improves access to great healthcare by applying transformational technologies like Cloud Computing and AI. Over the last 25 years in the software industry, Alexis has delivered products and services at startups and Fortune 100 companies. Prior to Dialogue, he led the design and development of social analytics system, built an award-winning semantic search engine, incubated a platform for conversational apps. Alexis has co-founded Pi Corp, a pioneering cloud startup and successfully sold it to EMC. An active member of Montreal's technology startup community, Alexis holds several software patents.
